## ðŸ” Discovery Deep-Dive (15-20 Minutes)

### The "Pain Excavation" Questions

**Start Broad:**
1. "What made you book this call today?"
2. "Tell me about your business and what you're working on."

**Go Deeper (The Gold):**
3. "What's the biggest bottleneck in your operation right now?"
4. "If you could wave a magic wand and fix ONE thing, what would it be?"
5. "How much time are you personally spending on [that bottleneck] per week?"
6. "What has that cost you in terms of missed opportunities?"

**Future State:**
7. "If we solved that, what does success look like 6 months from now?"
8. "What would getting that time back mean for you personally?"

**Decision Process:**
9. "If we found a solution that made sense, what would your timeline be?"
10. "Who else would need to be involved in making a decision?"

### Active Listening Signals

- "That's really helpful, tell me more about that."
- "I hear you saying [X], is that right?"
- "It sounds like [problem] has been frustrating. How long has that been going on?"

### Red Flags to Note

- No clear pain point (tire kicker)
- Won't share budget range (not serious)
- "I just want to learn about AI" (educator, not buyer)
- No decision authority (gatekeeper)
